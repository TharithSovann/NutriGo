About Us

In the efforts of making the cooking-at-home process easier, we decided to make a website, called NutriGo.

It focuses on the use of ingredients to be efficient and utilized. A web application that aims to solve obtaining dishes in a well-organized and keep ingredients from being wasted. NutriGo calculates the total ingredients needed to efficiently create the dishes you want.
